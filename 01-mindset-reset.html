03 · Myth Demolition

5 Myths That Are Holding You Back Right Now

Every one of these beliefs is common, understandable, and wrong. Replace each with the truth.

❌ MYTH 01

"AI will take my job"

The most common fear, and the most misframed. AI doesn't take jobs — it eliminates tasks. The jobs that survive are the ones that add what AI cannot: judgement, relationships, creativity, accountability.

✅ TRUTH 01

"AI will change my job — and I can shape how"

The professionals who will thrive are those who identify what AI handles, take it off their plate, and invest that recovered time into higher-value human work. You are not being replaced. You are being upgraded — if you choose to be.

❌ MYTH 02

"You need to be technical to use AI"

This was true in 2015. Today's AI tools are designed for conversation — you type what you want in plain language and it responds. If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can use AI.

✅ TRUTH 02

"You need curiosity, not a computer science degree"

The barrier is no longer technical. It is psychological. The people currently winning with AI are not engineers — they are curious professionals who started experimenting and didn't stop. The tools are already simple enough. The question is whether you'll start.

❌ MYTH 03

"AI is always right"

This is the most dangerous myth. AI "hallucinates" — it generates plausible-sounding information that is factually wrong, with complete confidence. Over-trusting AI without verification leads to embarrassing and sometimes costly errors.

✅ TRUTH 03

"AI is a powerful first draft — you are the editor"

Use AI to generate, research, draft, and brainstorm. Then apply your expertise and critical thinking to verify and refine. Your professional judgement is not replaced — it is elevated to quality control. You are the last human in the loop. Act like it matters.

❌ MYTH 04

"It's too late — everyone already knows AI"

The reality: the vast majority of professionals globally have no serious AI skills. Africa specifically has an enormous skills gap that represents either a crisis or an opportunity depending on how you respond to it.

✅ TRUTH 04

"The gap is wide open — and you're filling it right now"

The professional who finishes this masterclass will be in the top few percent of AI-literate professionals in their region. The gap isn't closing — it's widening. Starting today puts you ahead of where you were yesterday, and ahead of the majority who still haven't started.

❌ MYTH 05

"AI is just for big companies or Silicon Valley"

ChatGPT is free. Claude is free. Gemini is free. Canva AI is free at the basic tier. The tools that were exclusively available to companies with million-dollar tech budgets are now available to a secondary school teacher in Francistown on a smartphone.

✅ TRUTH 05

"AI is the great equaliser — if you use it"

This is the first time in history that a small business owner in Gaborone has access to the same AI productivity tools as a Fortune 500 company. That is an extraordinary equalisation of access. The only question is whether you'll use it.

04 · The Foundation

The AI Growth Mindset — What It Actually Looks Like

"You don't need to understand how the engine works to drive the car. But you do need to know where you're going."
— The right relationship with AI tools

❌ Fixed AI Mindset

  • "I tried it once, it didn't work"
  • "AI can't understand my specific context"
  • "I'll wait until it's better / more established"
  • "My industry is different"
  • "I'm too senior to be learning new tools"
  • "What if I do it wrong?"

✅ Growth AI Mindset

  • "I tried it once, let me refine my approach"
  • "Let me teach AI my context and see what happens"
  • "I'll start now and adapt as it evolves"
  • "What could this change in my industry?"
  • "The best leaders are always learning"
  • "What's the worst that can happen from experimenting?"

Exercise 01 · Individual

🎯 The One Fear, One Action Exercise

The research on behaviour change is clear: naming a specific fear and committing to a single concrete action is more effective than general motivation.

  1. Write your single biggest fear about AI in one sentence. Be specific and honest.
  2. Below it, write the single action — one thing, this week — that would directly reduce that fear.
  3. Tell one person about your commitment. Accountability doubles follow-through.

Exercise 02 · 10 Minutes Right Now

💬 Your First Conversation with AI

If you have never used ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini — do this before moving to Module 02.

  1. Go to chat.openai.com or claude.ai (free, no credit card required)
  2. Type: "I'm a [your profession] in [your country]. Give me 5 ways I could use AI in my work this week. Be specific and practical."
  3. Read the response. Notice what surprises you. Notice what's wrong or irrelevant.
  4. Type a follow-up: "The third suggestion doesn't apply to my context because [reason]. Give me a better alternative."
  5. Notice that you just had a professional conversation with an AI. That's prompting. You're already doing it.

Exercise 03 · Group · 30 Minutes

🎭 The Fear Parade

For workshop facilitators. Most powerful exercise in this module.

  1. Each participant writes their biggest AI fear on a sticky note (anonymous).
  2. Collect all notes. Read them aloud one by one.
  3. For each fear, the group calls out: "Me too!" if they share it.
  4. After all fears are named: facilitator asks — "Now that you can see everyone in this room shares these fears, what changes?"
  5. Key insight: normalising the fear removes the shame. Removing the shame is the first step to action.
💡

In 8 years of running workshops, this exercise consistently produces the biggest shift in the room. Isolation amplifies fear. Community dissolves it. You are not alone in this — and now everyone in the room knows it.

06 · Your Commitment

The 30-Day AI Fluency Challenge

📅 Week by Week

Week 1 — Explore: Use an AI tool for at least one work task per day. Just observe. No pressure to be efficient yet.
Week 2 — Apply: Complete Module 04 (Prompting). Use AI to produce one real deliverable — a proposal, a report, a lesson plan.
Week 3 — Integrate: Complete Module 05 (Automation). Identify and set up one workflow that saves you time every week.
Week 4 — Teach: Explain AI to one person who knows less than you. Teaching is the highest form of learning — and it builds your reputation.
