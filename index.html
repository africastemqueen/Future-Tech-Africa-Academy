Design Principles

Built Different

This curriculum is a direct rejection of AI courses that treat humans as an afterthought.

❤️

Humans First

AI is a tool. People are the point. Every lesson reinforces what makes humans irreplaceable.

🔨

Practical Over Theoretical

Every module ends with exercises you can do today. No abstract concepts without real-world application.

🌍

Africa-Relevant

Examples, case studies, and context from Botswana, Kenya, Nigeria, and across the continent.

🧭

Ethics is Non-Negotiable

Woven into every module — not a checkbox at the end. AI without ethics is not progress.

🔓

Radically Open

Free forever. CC BY 4.0. Share it, adapt it, translate it. Just attribute and keep it open.
