03 · The Online Course Playbook

Launch Your First Course on Systeme.io

Systeme.io has a free plan that includes courses, email marketing, and funnels. You can launch a fully functioning online course in 7 days — at zero cost.

📦 The 7-Day Course Launch Blueprint

1

Day 1 — Define & Name Your Course

Use AI: "Help me design a 6-module online course on [topic] for [audience]. Give me a compelling course name, 5 learning outcomes, and a module breakdown." Your existing expertise + this APEX AI Masterclass content = your first product.

2

Day 2 — Create Your Systeme.io Account & Course Shell

Go to systeme.io, create a free account. Under "Courses", create a new course. Add your modules and lesson titles. Upload a cover image (create free on Canva). Set your price.

3

Day 3–4 — Record Your Content

You don't need a studio. Phone camera + ring light (BWP 200–400) + quiet room = professional enough. Aim for 5–15 minute lessons. Use AI to write your scripts: "Write a 10-minute lesson script on [topic] for my online course. Include: key concept, 1 example, 1 exercise, recap."

4

Day 5 — Build Your Sales Page

In Systeme.io, use their sales page builder. Use AI: "Write a compelling sales page for an online course called [name] targeting [audience]. Include: headline, 5 pain points, 5 outcomes, instructor bio, 3 testimonials (placeholder), pricing, and 3 FAQs." Edit for your voice.

5

Day 6 — Set Up Payment & Email

Connect PayPal or Stripe (or Paychangu for Botswana — accepts MoMo and cards). Set up a simple 3-email welcome sequence in Systeme.io: Day 1 (welcome), Day 3 (quick win), Day 7 (check-in). AI writes all three in 20 minutes.

6

Day 7 — Launch to Your Existing Network First

Do NOT start with Facebook ads. Start with warm outreach to 20 people who already know and trust you. Offer a 30% founding-student discount. Your first 5 sales will come from people who already believe in you. Use their feedback to improve before scaling.

💡

The Pricing Decision for Online Courses

African market pricing reality: BWP 200–500 courses attract more African buyers. USD 297–997 courses attract international and diaspora buyers, NGO/corporate bulk purchases, and build more perceived value. The right move is often a "local access" price and a "global" price for the same course — Systeme.io allows multiple pricing tiers. Do not price yourself out of your home market, but also do not anchor your global price to local purchasing power.

04 · Corporate Client Acquisition

How to Land Corporate Training Contracts

🎯 Your Target Clients

  • Financial sector: Banks, insurance, microfinance — all navigating AI compliance
  • Mining & resources: Botswana's economic bedrock, increasing automation
  • Government ministries: Digital transformation mandates across SADC
  • Education sector: Universities, colleges, educator professional development
  • NGOs & donor orgs: USAID, World Bank, GIZ all fund digital literacy
  • Healthcare: AI in diagnostics, admin, patient communication

📬 The Outreach Formula

  • Step 1: Identify the HR Director or L&D Manager (LinkedIn)
  • Step 2: Research their company's AI challenge (annual report, news)
  • Step 3: Send a personalised email: problem insight + your solution + one relevant result
  • Step 4: Offer a free "AI Readiness Assessment" (30 min call)
  • Step 5: Convert assessment to paid workshop proposal
  • Step 6: Deliver, over-deliver, ask for referral + testimonial

05 · The Action Plan

Your 90-Day Monetisation Sprint

90 days is enough time to go from zero to your first paying students, first corporate inquiry, and first speaking appearance. Structured below — execute consistently.

1
Days 1–30: Foundation & First Revenue
Month 1
  • Week 1: Define your 2 primary revenue streams. Research 20 target corporate clients. Build your LinkedIn profile for credibility (use AI to optimise).
  • Week 2: Create your Systeme.io account. Map your first online course structure. Record Module 1 of your course.
  • Week 3: Send 10 personalised corporate outreach emails. Set up your course sales page. Record Modules 2–3.
  • Week 4: Follow up on outreach. Launch your course to your warm network with a founding discount. Target: 5 paying students.
  • Target revenue: BWP 5,000–15,000 (founding course sales + one workshop inquiry)
2
Days 31–60: Build the Engine
Month 2
  • Week 5: Complete and launch full course. Start publishing 2x LinkedIn posts per week on AI topics — use AI to generate, edit to your voice.
  • Week 6: Deliver your first corporate presentation or workshop (even free/discounted to build case study). Document everything for testimonials.
  • Week 7: Apply to speak at one local or regional event. Submit a conference abstract. Update your speaker bio using AI.
  • Week 8: Create a simple AI Starter Toolkit digital product. Price at BWP 150–250. Promote to existing audience and new connections.
  • Target revenue: BWP 20,000–50,000 (corporate training + course sales + digital product)
3
Days 61–90: Scale & Systemise
Month 3
  • Week 9: Raise your corporate training rate by 20–30% for new enquiries. Convert your best workshop into a proposal template with AI assistance.
  • Week 10: Launch your first group coaching cohort (8–12 people, 6 weeks, USD 300–500 per person). Use WhatsApp + Systeme.io for delivery.
  • Week 11: Ask every happy client for 2 referrals and 1 testimonial. Build your referral flywheel — it becomes your best acquisition channel.
  • Week 12: Review your 90-day results. Calculate your monthly recurring revenue. Plan your next 90 days with a higher revenue target.
  • Target revenue: BWP 50,000–120,000 cumulative from all streams
